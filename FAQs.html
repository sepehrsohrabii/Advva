Advva for Businesses

How will Advva measure results of advertising campaigns?

We carefully track the mileage of our sponsored drivers to make sure your business gets abundant exposure every day. Our moving ads convert like crazy because your business will receive anywhere from 10-20X more exposure compared to stationary billboards in a crowded area.

What makes Advva different from other advertising companies?

Our unique data driven campaigns are far superior to ordinary digital advertising methods for three reasons:
1- Potential customers can’t skip, click out of, or avoid the ads displayed on the vehicles of our sponsored drivers.
2- Your ads get 24/7 exposure every day of the week, even when vehicles are parked.
3- We compensate our sponsored drivers well , so they are heavily motivated to drive at least 1,000 miles every month which brings in more revenue for you and for them.

Is my business responsible for any damages to the vehicle?

Our unique vehicle friendly adhesive causes absolutely no damage to the paint job on any vehicle. Additionally, all of our wraps are installed by certified body shop professionals located nearby.

Why should I partner with you instead of advertising on billboards?

Our sponsored drivers often travel on packed city streets all day long, so your company will be exposed to more customers in less time. Also, our company charges way less than the overpriced fees of expensive billboards while generating more revenue every month for your business.
